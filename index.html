 About Ulka Industries Private Limited :

Ulka Group of Companies was established in the year 1999 by Mr.B.B Nikam. He was a brilliant Mill Engineer and had worked as a chief Engineer with various Sugar factories for more than 16 years.

The Ulka Group Of Companies is now managed by enterprising Engineering & Management Professional Dr. Sachin B Nikam, Managing Director and ably supported by Mr. Dhiraj B Nikam, Director and Mr. Vipul Kulkarni, CEO.

Ulka Industries Private Limited is dedicated to Cane Milling Technology and supply of complete sugar plant on Trunkey basis. The group has more than 20 National & Internnational Patents to its credit. Ulka is known for its high performance and energy efficient Equipments for the Cane Sugar Industry. We are one of the leading Manufacturer and Erector of Sugar Mill Machinery. Our Office is fully equipped with the Latest Software Systems and Communication Facilities. The Design and Engineering Department is staffed to provide services for preparation of Designs and Drawings of Sugar Plant Equipment and for Sugar Plant projects.

We have modern Workshop and Quality Control and Quality Assurance facilities at our Bhosari works.
 
Our Engineering efforts are aimed at
  • Saving of Energy
  • Optimum Efficiency
  • Reduction of capital costs
  • Enhanced Profitability
